Hetjur

Í Overwatch eru spilalegu kallarnir kallaðar hetjur. Einmitt Núna eru 23 Hetjur í leiknum hægta að spila. Hetjurnar eru brotnar niður í fjóra hópa. Offense, Defense, Tank og Support. Í flestum leikjum muna vera tvær hetjur frá offense eða defense hópnum, tveir Tanks og tveir Support. Offense og Defense hetjurnar eru oft Kallaðar "DPS" hetjur, því starf þeirra er að gera mikin skaða í stuttum tíma

Offense Heroes

Genji

Genji Is a mobile assassin who wants to get behind the enemies line and take them one by one.

Mccree

McCree Is a mid range shooter who wants to try to deal as much damage as he can with precise aiming

Pharah

Phara Fires rockets from above to disrupt and deal as much damage from the air as she can

Reaper

Reaper Is a short range hero who wants to Flank the enemy and suprise them from there blind spot

Soldier 76

Soldier is very basic where he just wants to deal alot of damage from mid range to slowly take people down

Sombra

Sombra is a disruptor who´s main job is to be as anyoing as she can while she flanks and disrupts the enemy

Tracer

Tracer is a disruptor who´s main job is to be as anyoing as she can while trying to kill the back line.

Defense Heroes

Bastion

Bastion is a champion who deals alot of damage while his mobility is very low

Hanzo

Hanzo is a mid range sniper who wants to deal alot of damage while trying to headshot people

Junkrat

Junkrat fires grenades who are hard to hit but deal alot of damage and disrupt the enemy

Mei

Mei disrupts or blocks the enemy while slowing and stuning the enemy

Torbjorn

Torbjörn Builds turrets to defend a point while giving his allies armor

Widowmaker

Widowmaker is a long range sniper who wants to try to headshot the squishies

Tank Heroes

D.Va

D.Va is a fair damage dealer who wants to go for the back line kill a target and get back out.

Reinhardt

Reinhardt is a tank who uses a giant shield to protect his allies.

Roadhog

Roadhog is a bruizer who wants to be in the middle of the fight while not dying

Winston

Winston is a tank who either wants to try to find 1vs1 or zerg the payload

Zarya

Zarya is a bruizer who wants to be in the middle of the fight while soaking in damage with her shields to build up damage

Support Heroes

Ana

Ana is a healer/damage dealer who provides a little bit of everything for the team

Lúcio

Lúcio provides either healing or movement speed for his allies that are around him. And can provide a big shield for everyone around him when needed

Mercy

Mercy is a single target healer or damage boost who can ress her allies.

Symmetra

Symmetra puts down alot of turrets that deal damage and slow. She can also put down a teleporter for her team mates

Zenyatta

Symmetra puts down alot of turrets that deal damage and slow. She can also put down a teleporter for her team mates
